About Purple Couch Bookshop

Purple Couch Bookshop is an independent bookstore located at the North Andover Mall. We're passionate about connecting readers with great books and creating a welcoming community space for book lovers of all ages.

Whether you're looking for the latest bestseller, a hidden gem, or a special gift, our knowledgeable staff is here to help you find exactly what you need. We also carry Warhammer miniatures and games for the tabletop gaming enthusiasts!
