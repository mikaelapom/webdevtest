Part of our mission is to promote curiosity and a love of learning about the world around us. With that comes a desire to find new books and new creations that we love, that astound us, that make us think, that make us feel, and that leave us wanting more.

We aspire to be known as a place that shares local talent and keeps local creativity alive amidst the big box stores and algorithmically generated experiences. We want to showcase the works of authors and artisans from the Merrimack Valley and surrounding area.

Please tell us a bit about you, what you create, and how you see us working together to promote it.

Please note:

  • We provide limited space for local artisans on a consignment basis.
  • Independently-published authors must be available through Ingram.
